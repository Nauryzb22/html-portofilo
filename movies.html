1+1

“1+1” ( French Intouchables - “The Untouchables” ) is a 2011 French comedy drama based on real events about the successful aristocrat Philip, who As a result of an accident, he ends up in a wheelchair and takes on a black former petty criminal, Driss, as an assistant. The main roles are played by François Cluzet and Omar Sy , who was awarded the national Cesar Award for this acting work . The premiere in France took place on November 2 , 2011 . In Russia, the film was released on April 26, 2012 under the title “1+1” .

Interstellar

“Interstellar” ( eng. Interstellar - “Interstellar” ) is a science fiction film directed by Christopher Nolan from a script co-written with Jonathan Nolan . The film follows the journeys of a group of explorers who use a newly discovered space-time tunnel to bypass the limitations of human space flight and conquer vast distances in an interstellar ship. The screenwriter for the project was Christopher's brother, Jonathan Nolan, who worked on the scripts for two films in the Batman trilogy , as well as the films Memento and The Prestige . The script is based on the scientific works of Kip Thorne .
